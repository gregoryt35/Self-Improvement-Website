Physical

In this section, we explore topics used to improve your physical health.

● Exercise

The first thing we urge everyone to do is some form of exercise. Exercise is essential, not only for the body, but for the mind as well. We recommend doing at least one physical activity each day. This could be anything, such as walking, running, swimming, biking, weight lifting, playing a sport, etc. As long as you are getting out there and being active, you are taking a step in the right direction.

● Clean eating

With so many options out there, from Big-Macs to donuts, one must be sure to maintain a healthy diet. One of the biggest issues that plague our society is unhealthy eating habits. It is imperative that you eat a well-balanced diet of nutritious foods, such as high-quality fruits, vegetables, meats, etc. Also, make sure to drink plenty of water. We understand that it may not be sustainable to eat clean all the time, so eating out on occassion is fine, as long as it does not become the main driver of your diet.

● Sleep

Sleep, something a lot of us can agree we do not get enough of. Despite this, sleep is arguably the most important thing to keep in check. Lack of sleep causes a myriad of issues, no matter how much you exercise or how clean your diet is. Medical professionals recommend anywhere from 7-9 hours of sleep per night for optimal health.
