Here are some ways you can reduce stress and break free

● Improve your time management

The first and most obvious way to reduce stress is to manage your time well. We understand the urge to procrastinate and have fun first, but nothing is really enjoyable when you have something else in the back of your mind. One way to improve your time management is to create a plan. One thing to keep in mind when making this plan is to make a priority list, not a to-do list. Think about what needs to be done and prioritize the most critical tasks. Futhermore, set deadlines for yourself and break large tasks into smaller, more doable chunks.

● Meditate

Almost everyone has heard of meditation but almost no one actually practices it. However, when we meditate, we lower our stress levels, improve focus, and become more mindful. After a successful meditation session, one becomes much more relaxed. You can see how this improves mental health. For starters, just do some deep breathing in a calm and quiet environment. Your mind may wander but be sure to not engage with those thoughts and focus simply on your breath.

● Talk to someone

When all else doesn't work, you can always talk to someone. After all, humans are inherently social creatures. Whether it's a friend, family member, or even therapist, venting to someone you trust can always help you with your problems. They can shut up and simply listen, or even give you advice if you want. It's always nice to see the situation from another perspective.
